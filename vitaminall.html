Vitaminall Rebrand

A rebrand for a growing audience

Overview

This was a personal project where I implemented a rebrand of the YouTube channel for the streamer Vitaminall, whose content focused on playing Fortnite.

Project Details

Project Length

10 weeks

Project Role

Graphic Designer

Deliverables

  • Smaller Icon for Account Profile
  • Banner for Channel Homepage

Research

Because this YouTube channel focused on creating niche content. I first conducted an informal competitive analysis of other popular channels that streamed this kind of content such as Ninja and Myth.

After this research I drew some rough sketches that had a similar animated style to fortnite and some of the other channels that I studied while also incorporating the pill/medicine theme that Vitaminall already had. In this case I added a doctor’s reflector headband, a bandolier with pills and in some versions a large needle.

Ideation

My first attempt at creating a cartoon-like design in Adobe Illustrator came from utilizing simple vector shapes to recreate some ideas from the original sketches that I had made.

However I saw that this version was too simplistic to use for the rebrand so I began thinking of how I could incorporate more detail and style into these digital designs The solution I ended up going with was to sketch in more detail on paper, then scan it into a png then using illustrator trace the design to create a SVG I could manipulate.

Final Designs

Having established the groundwork for the rebrand with the character sketch I moved onto talking with the YouTuber so that I could get a better sense of what he wanted in terms of color and style outside of the character/logo. After weeks of working with him I was able to help him find the color scheme that spoke to his personality and what he wanted his audience to perceive.

Reflection

Through this project I was able to learn:

  • How to use empathy and communicate with the client to help achieve someone else’s vision.
  • How to create more detailed drawings and incorporate them into digital designs.
