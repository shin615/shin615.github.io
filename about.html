About Me

Hello, my name is Seung Won. I am currently a senior at the University of Washington studying Human Centered Design and Engineering. This major focuses on the growing field of UX Design and how we can create better interactions between technology and people.

My current professional goal is to graduate and find work as a full time UX Designer.

For background, I was born in South Korea but I grew up in the Pacific Northwest. As a part of the 1.5 immigrant generation I have always been able to look at the world with viewpoints from the culture I grew up in and the culture I originally came from. I have continued to cultivate and utilize this skill in my journey to becoming a UX Designer. As I create new user experiences I always work to see and experience them from the view of the users and stakeholders.

My current favorite are of UX design is cross cultural design because it speaks to my background as a person who is a part of two different cultures and how different traditions and ideas can be adapted and combined to create something unique for each country.

My hobbies and interests include making music and reading.

I look forward to working with you.
